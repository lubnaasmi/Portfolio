My Story

My journey into data science began with a curiosity about how information can reveal patterns, shape decisions, and inspire innovation. With a background in Computer Science, I discovered the power of combining analytical thinking and creativity to transform raw data into meaningful insights. Over the years, I’ve built skills across Python, SQL, Machine Learning, and AI-driven analytics — developing models, dashboards, and intelligent solutions that bring clarity to complexity. I’m passionate about using data to solve real-world problems, empower people, and make technology more human-centered. Every project I take on is an opportunity to learn, experiment, and create something that has lasting impact.

My Toolkit

I’m skilled in Python, SQL, Tableau, and Machine Learning, with hands-on experience in data cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA), data modeling, and dashboard development. I specialize in transforming raw, complex data into clear, actionable insights through analytical thinking and creative problem-solving. My work combines technical precision with a human-centered approach — using AI, visualization, and data storytelling to drive smarter, more meaningful decisions.

Beyond the Data

Volunteering for charity organizations keeps me grounded and reminds me why I value community, empathy, and impact. For me, learning isn’t limited to code or models — it’s about engaging with life, staying curious, and finding inspiration in everyday experiences.
